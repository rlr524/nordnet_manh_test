Online Injury and Incident Reporting

Stores and offices, other than Manhattan, must continue to report all employee injuries to your human resources office, do not report employee injuries via this online tool. Only the Manhattan store and warehouses (DC, NQC, and Fulfillment) may use this online reporting tool at this time.

To comply with state and federal safety regulations, Risk Management must be notified immediately when an employee is severely injured or is transported by ambulance to a medical facility. If the injury occurs after normal business hours, use the "Risk Management" Outlook mailbox (askrisk@nordstrom.com) to report the injury in addition to the usual employee injury reporting process, or contact any on-call emergency contact. Risk Management will investigate and report the injury as needed. Failure to report timely may lead to government levied fines/penalties.
